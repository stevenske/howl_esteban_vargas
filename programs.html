Programs and Tours
Night Tour

Visit Howl Park in the evening and see our animals in a whole new light. The wolves become lively as the night falls and the air cools.

Pack Tour

Join the classic tour of Howl Park, where one of our education docents leads you through the park.

Private Tour

Join one of our highly trained education docents for a private guided tour to see the gray wolves, red foxes, gray foxes, and bison that call Howl Park home.

Winter Tour

Visit the Howl Park in winter, where one of our education docents leads you through the park and show's you how the wolves survive in this season

Howl Camping

Enjoy the night camping with the beautiful howls of the wolves and a hot chocolate for the cold winds.

Feeding Tour

Join our animal care staff during feeding time to learn about the diet, hunting strategies, and behavioral ecology of wolves.
